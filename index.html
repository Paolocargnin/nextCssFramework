We validate email addresses

For Managers

1. Upload your list of email addresses

Verifalia is a web-based email validation service which allows to upload and validate lists of email addresses with ease. To scrub your lists, just upload one or more files with the addresses to validate and our service will detect the invalid and non-working ones, giving back a detailed report.

2. Get the validation results

The report for your email validation will be very detailed and contain every information you need to update your database, including deliverability status, network error codes, disposable email address (DEA) detection result and much more. Reports can be easily downloaded and saved to your computer for further analysis.

For Developers

Integrate your own app

Software developers love to integrate Verifalia within their software applications, as the whole email validation service is exposed via an elegant and secure REST API, which uses JSON as its primary data interchange format. A couple of lines of code and Verifalia will validate email addresses from within your own applications!
